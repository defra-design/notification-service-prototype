Before the consignment is imported

You still need to:

  • upload the ITAHC and any other required documents
  • complete the animal identifier information in the notification

Transporting the consignment

Tell the transporter that this consignment must go directly to the place of destination on entry to Great Britain.

The animals or consignment may need to be inspected.

If so, the animals must stay at the place of destination for either:

  • the time stated on the health certificate
  • 48 hours if no time is specified
